Breakfast-Sausages with Egg

Jimmy Dean Pork Sausages- Cut them invo round small pieces and fry them in the fry pan along with Egg pouches. This is a healthy breakfast and will keep you full for at least couple of hours.

Lunch-Beef Stakes with Salad

Get some beef sausages andcut them small pieces. Cook them into a frypan. Fry them according to your preference (Medium or rare). Serve them with some fresh salad.
